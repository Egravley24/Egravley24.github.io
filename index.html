Services

What We Offer 

Clay and Wheels

After you pay our studio fee, you have access to 2.5 logs of clay for that month! We also have enough wheels for 30 people at a time! 

 Glaze

We also have mixed glazes available, that are included in your studio fee! Underglazes are not available, and if you are using them you will have to purchase them yourself. 

Kilns

We also have a variety of kilns, including and cone 10, four cone 6, and a raku kiln!

About

  • January 2025

    Our Humble Beginnings

    In January of 2025 our founder began taking Ceramics 1 from her university, Bob Jones University. In this spring semester she learned the ropes of pottery, and made many mugs, bowls, and other trinkets. 

  • May 2025 

    An Agency is Born

    In may our founder opened the doors of our lovely pottery studio. This studio is a place where potters can come together and create masterpieces and fun pots.
